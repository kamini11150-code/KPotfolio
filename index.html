>How We Assist

Evidence Sharing: Upon official request, we provide detailed evidence such as:

  • Web pages with time/date stamps
  • MLM compensation plans
  • Product or service information (if any)
  • Details of directors, promoters, stakeholders, and founder members
  • Relevant media
  • Policies, procedures, and terms and conditions.

Early Warning Mechanisms

We maintain an early detection and warning system for deceptive and unsustainable multi- level marketing (MLM) entities, helping law enforcement take preventive action before further harm occurs.

Legal Reference

Our evidence and reports reference relevant laws, including but not limited to:

  • Prize Chits and Money Circulation (Banning) Act, 1978
  • IPC 420
  • Protection of the Interest of Depositors Act (State-specific)
  • Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002
  • Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act, 2019.
  • Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023:
    We now also reference the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023, which replaces the Indian Penal Code and includes updated provisions relevant to financial fraud, cheating, and offenses related to MLM operations.
